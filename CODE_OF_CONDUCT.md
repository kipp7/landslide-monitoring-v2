# Code Of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and specific.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the work.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
